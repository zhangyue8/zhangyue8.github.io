Why is Japan prone to earthquakes?

Japan sits on the most active seismic belt in the world, the Pacific ring of earthquakes.
Earthquake, also known as ground motion, ground vibration, is the crust of the rapid release of energy caused by the process of vibration, during the earthquake waves that will produce a natural phenomenon.
The main cause of earthquakes is the collision between the plates and the edges of the plates.
Earthquake began to occur at the site known as the source, the source is above the ground known as the epicenter.
The ground of destructive earthquake vibration is the strongest place called extremely seismic area, extremely seismic area is often the epicenter of the area.
Earthquake often cause serious casualties, can cause fire, flood, toxic gas leakage, bacteria, and radioactive materials spread, may also cause tsunami, landslide, collapse, cracks and other secondary disasters.
According to statistics, the earth every year about more than 5 million earthquakes, that is, every day to have tens of thousands of earthquakes.
Most are too small or too far away to be felt; There are about ten or twenty earthquakes that can cause serious harm to human beings.
There are about one or two earthquakes that can cause particularly severe damage.
Earthquakes that cannot be felt must be recorded with seismometers. Different types of seismometers can record earthquakes of different intensities at different distances.
Thousands of different kinds of seismic instruments operate around the world to monitor the movement of earthquakes day and night.
